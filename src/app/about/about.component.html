About

Train your Brain helps you in learning new facts and knowledge. Add new questions testing your brain power and the correct answer to validate your finesse.

Home

On the home page you will be greeted with your questions. You can choose to skip the question or, if you know the solution, display the answer and check for yourself if you were right. But don't trick yourself ;).

Add Question

Here you are able to add new facts and questions you want to train your brain on. Provide your question and answer and off you go. If you need to include images as your question or answer you also have this option. Please note that you still have to provide some text for your question and answer.

See all Questions

Scroll through all of your questions, see how you perform on each question or delete an item you already know for sure.
